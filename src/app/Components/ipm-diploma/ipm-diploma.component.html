International IPM Professional Diploma

International IPM Professional Diploma is a joint programme between Egypt and Turkey, in collaboration between AHDO and BioPheroPoint and several collaborations with both Egyptian and Turkish Universities, Institutions and Pre-harvest and; Post-harvest food businesses.

Study Start Date: September 2023

Duration: 9 Months ( 2 semisters)

If interested in the Cross Training Scholarships in Turkey, Kindly note that you have to pass with success the English Programme included in the Diploma programme in addition to the interview process.
Country of study
  • Egypt
  • Turkey
    (for accepted applications for the cross training programme)
 Studying Fees :
  • Study in Turkey is FREE of charge including the accommodation
  • Study in Egypt fees: EGP 24300 (For Egypt Residents),
    USD 2250 (For Egypt non-residents).
