International Professional Diploma in Food Safety and Quality

International Professional Diploma in Food Safety and Quality is a joint programme between Egypt and Turkey, in collaboration between AHDO, Konya Food and Agriculture University, BiopheroPoint, and several collaborations with both Egyptian and Turkish Universities, Institutions and Food and Beverages establishments..

Deadline: 15th of July 2023

Study Start Date: September 2023

Duration: 9 Months ( 2 semisters)

If interested in the Cross Training Scholarships in Turkey, Kindly note that you have to pass with success the English Programme included in the Diploma programme in addition to the interview process.

 Who can attend?
Relevant Educational and/or Relevant Work Experience as the following:

  • Relevant Education as:
    • University/institute Bachelor or Diploma graduates
    • Last year students
    • However, the following studies are recommended:
    • Veterinary Medicine
    • Science
    • Pharmacy
    • Education
    • Agriculture
    • Tourism
    • Other related high studies level
  • Relevant Work Experience as:
    • Food Safety Managers or supervisors or already working in the food manufacturing, catering, and retailings
    • Staff working in competent authorities or enforcement; for example, National Food Safety Authorities and/or Environmental Health Officers, or other related food and agricultural authorities
    • Staff in diagnostic or food microbiology, chemical laboratories
